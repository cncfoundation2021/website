Sweets Manufacturing

Traditional and modern sweets made with the finest ingredients and traditional recipes

Traditional Sweets

We specialize in manufacturing traditional Assamese and Indian sweets using age-old recipes and premium ingredients.

  • Rasgulla - Soft cottage cheese balls in sugar syrup
  • Gulab Jamun - Deep-fried milk solids in rose-flavored syrup
  • Ladoo - Sweet balls made from various ingredients
  • Barfi - Dense milk-based sweets in various flavors
  • Halwa - Semolina or flour-based sweet pudding
  • Jalebi - Deep-fried spiral sweets soaked in syrup

Modern Sweets

Contemporary sweet varieties that blend traditional techniques with modern flavors and presentation.

  • Chocolate Sweets - Premium chocolate-based confections
  • Dry Fruit Sweets - Rich sweets with nuts and dried fruits
  • Fusion Sweets - Innovative combinations of traditional and modern
  • Sugar-free Options - Diabetic-friendly sweet alternatives
  • Packaged Sweets - Ready-to-eat packaged varieties
  • Custom Designs - Personalized sweets for special occasions

Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility ensures consistent quality and hygiene standards.

  • Quality Ingredients: Premium milk, sugar, and natural flavors
  • Hygiene Standards: FSSAI certified production facility
  • Traditional Methods: Time-tested recipes and techniques
  • Modern Equipment: Automated processes for consistency
  • Quality Control: Multi-level quality checks
  • Packaging: Airtight packaging for freshness

Product Range

Wide variety of sweets catering to different tastes and occasions.

  • Festival Specials: Diwali, Eid, Durga Puja collections
  • Wedding Sweets: Custom packages for ceremonies
  • Corporate Gifts: Branded sweet boxes for businesses
  • Retail Packs: Individual and family-sized packages
  • Bulk Orders: Wholesale quantities for retailers

Quality Assurance

Comprehensive quality control measures ensure the highest standards in every batch.

  • FSSAI License: Food safety and standards compliance
  • HACCP Certification: Hazard analysis and critical control points
  • ISO 22000: Food safety management system
  • Regular Testing: Laboratory testing of ingredients and products
  • Traceability: Complete tracking from ingredients to finished product
  • Customer Feedback: Continuous improvement based on feedback

Distribution & Availability

Our sweets are available through multiple channels across Assam and neighboring states.

  • Retail Outlets: 50+ authorized retail stores
  • Online Orders: Home delivery through our website
  • Wholesale: Supply to hotels, restaurants, and retailers
  • Export: International distribution to select countries
  • Corporate Orders: Bulk orders for events and festivals
  • Fresh Daily: Daily production for maximum freshness
